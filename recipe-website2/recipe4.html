Caesar Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2 people

Difficulty: Easy

Instructions

  1. Cut the bread into small cubes. Mix with olive oil, garlic powder and a little salt. Bake in oven at 190 degrees for 12-15 minutes until crunchy and golden. Let it cool.
  2. Make the dressing by mixing mayonnaise, parmesan cheese, lemon juice, dijon mustard, worcestershire sauce, garlic and black pepper together in a small bowl. Taste and add more salt if needed.
  3. Wash the romaine lettuce and dry it properly. Wet lettuce will make the dressing watery. Tear it into pieces and put in a large bowl.
  4. Pour the dressing over the lettuce and toss well so every leaf gets coated.
  5. Add the croutons and parmesan on top. Serve straight away. Do not let it sit too long or it will get soggy.

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce
  • 40g parmesan cheese
  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Dressing:
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • Black pepper
  • Salt to taste
