Pasta Aglio e Olio

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 2 people

Difficulty: Easy

Instructions

  1. Boil a big pot of water with salt. Cook the spaghetti according to the packet instructions. Before draining save about 1 cup of the pasta water.
  2. While pasta is cooking, slice the garlic very thin. Put a pan on low heat and add the olive oil and garlic. Let it cook slowly for about 5 minutes. Do not let it go brown or it will taste bitter.
  3. Add the chilli flakes to the pan and stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the drained pasta into the pan. Pour some of the saved pasta water and mix everything together for 1-2 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Turn off heat. Add parsley, salt and pepper. Mix once more and serve right away.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • Handful of parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup pasta water
