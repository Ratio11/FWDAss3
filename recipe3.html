Gulab Jamun

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 20 pieces

Difficulty: Medium

Instructions

  1. First make the syrup. Add sugar and water in a pot and boil until sugar dissolves. Then add cardamom pods and rose water. Keep it warm on very low heat.
  2. For the dough mix milk powder, flour, baking soda and cardamom powder together in a bowl. Add ghee and rub it in with your fingers.
  3. Slowly add warm milk little by little and mix until you get a soft smooth dough. Do not add too much milk. Let the dough rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into 20 small equal pieces. Roll each piece into a smooth round ball. Make sure there are no cracks on the surface.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan on LOW heat. This is very important. The oil should not be too hot otherwise the outside will cook but inside will remain raw. Fry the balls slowly for 8-10 minutes turning them constantly until they are dark golden brown.
  6. Take the fried balls out and put them straight into the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 1 hour before eating.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 4-5 tbsp warm milk
  • Oil for frying
  • Syrup:
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 cup water
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp rose water
