Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Baking Time: 35 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour

Servings: 10 people

Difficulty: Hard

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees. Grease two round cake pans and line with baking paper.
  2. In a big bowl sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Mix together.
  3. In another bowl whisk sugar, eggs, oil, buttermilk and vanilla until combined.
  4. Add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients bowl and mix until combined. Then carefully add the hot coffee or hot water and stir. The batter will look very runny but that is okay.
  5. Pour the batter equally into the two cake pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Check by poking a toothpick in the middle, if it comes out clean the cake is done. Let cakes cool completely.
  6. For the ganache, heat the cream in a pot until it just starts bubbling. Pour the hot cream over the chopped chocolate. Wait 2 minutes then stir until smooth. Add butter and mix. Let it cool until thick enough to spread.
  7. Put first cake layer on a plate. Spread ganache on top. Place second layer on top and cover the top and sides with remaining ganache.
  8. Decorate however you like and serve. Cut with a sharp knife.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 300g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 120ml vegetable oil
  • 240ml buttermilk
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 240ml hot coffee
  • Ganache:
  • 300g dark chocolate
  • 300ml heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
