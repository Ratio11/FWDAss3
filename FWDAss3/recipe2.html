Butter Chicken

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Medium

Instructions

  1. Mix yoghurt, lemon juice, garam masala, chilli powder, turmeric, ginger garlic paste and salt in a bowl. Add chicken pieces and mix well. Leave to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan on high heat for about 5-6 minutes each side until it has some char on it. Keep aside.
  3. In a big pan melt 2 tbsp butter on medium heat. Add cardamom, cloves and cinnamon. Then add onions and fry until golden brown, around 10 minutes. Add ginger garlic paste and cook 2 more minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree and crushed tomatoes. Add chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin and a pinch of sugar. Cook for 15 minutes on low flame until sauce becomes thick.
  5. Let the sauce cool a little then blend it smooth using a blender.
  6. Put blended sauce back in pan on low heat. Add cream and remaining butter. Add the cooked chicken and simmer for 8 minutes. Add kasuri methi at the end and mix well.
  7. Serve hot with naan bread or rice.

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp yoghurt
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 onions chopped
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 150ml cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
